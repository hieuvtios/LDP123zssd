Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TCG Card Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using TCG Card Scanner, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Camera & Images

TCG Card Scanner uses your device camera solely to scan and identify trading cards. Images captured during a scan are processed locally on your device or transmitted temporarily to our recognition service. We do not store your photos on our servers after recognition is complete.

Card Collection Data

If you save cards to your collection, that data (card names, sets, quantities, condition) is stored locally on your device. If cloud backup is enabled, this data is synced to secure cloud storage associated with your account.

Account Information (Optional)

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Authentication credentials (stored securely, never in plain text)

Usage & Analytics Data

We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app, including:

  • Features used and scan frequency (no card images retained)
  • App crashes and error reports
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • General region (country-level, not precise location)

Advertising Data

The free version of TCG Card Scanner may display ads served by third-party ad networks (e.g., Google AdMob). These networks may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data subject to their own privacy policies. You may opt out via your device's ad-tracking settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify and price trading cards using our recognition engine
  • To sync and back up your card collection (if cloud backup is enabled)
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app
  • To diagnose and fix bugs and performance issues
  • To communicate important updates or support responses
  • To display relevant advertisements (free tier only)

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Card recognition APIs — temporary image data sent for identification purposes only
  • Cloud storage providers — for optional collection backup (e.g., Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Analytics providers — anonymized crash and usage data (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Ad networks — device identifiers for ad serving on the free tier
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Retention

Scan images are not retained after processing. Account data is kept as long as your account is active. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 24 months. You may delete your account and associated data at any time via the app settings or by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics or advertising tracking
  • Data portability (export your collection)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at thanetflix77@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

TCG Card Scanner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (TLS encryption, secure authentication, access controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
